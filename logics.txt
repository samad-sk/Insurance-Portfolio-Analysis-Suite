### 3️⃣ Why this is happening:

  [
  \text{Premium / Coverage} = \frac{6}{600} = 1%
  ]
  Normally, insurers price premiums as **a small % of coverage**, but usually enough to cover expected claims + admin + profit.

* If your **policies are underpriced**, even small claims can exceed the total premiums.

### 4️⃣ Insight:

* A **high claim ratio (>100%)** is not unusual if:
